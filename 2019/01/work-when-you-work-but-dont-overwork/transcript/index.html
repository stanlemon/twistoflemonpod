Episode Summary

The conversation revolves around John Colmire's career journey, including his experiences with finding meaning in work, dealing with high expectations, and recognizing that one person cannot hold everything together. He shares insights on the importance of self-awareness, the dangers of defining oneself by a job, and the value of having multiple roles or identities. The discussion also touches on the concept of emotional labor and the need to prioritize personal well-being.

  • Career Identity

  • High Expectations
  • Job Satisfaction
